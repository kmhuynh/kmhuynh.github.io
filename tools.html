Optimal Shrinkage Denoising

Breakthrough noise reduction for high-resolution diffusion MRI. 11x noise floor reduction, multi-channel complex-valued data processing, optimal shrinkage of singular values.

Language: MATLAB

OSF Repository Paper

If you use this tool, please cite:
Huynh, K. M., Chang, W. T., Wu, Y., & Yap, P. T. (2024). Optimal shrinkage denoising breaks the noise floor in high-resolution diffusion MRI. Patterns, 5(3), 100954.

24-Shell Diffusion MRI Data

Healthy adult 24-shell diffusion MRI dataset. Preprocessed with dcm2niix, eddy and topup via dwifslpreproc. See data.json for acquisition parameters. Available under MIT License.
