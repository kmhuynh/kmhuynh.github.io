I am a postdoc in radiology at UNC Chapel Hill. Since 2017, I've been working with <a href="https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=QGdnthwAAAAJ&hl=en" target="_blank">Dr. Pew-Thian Yap</a> on MR reconstruction, signal representation, noise removal, and microstructure imaging using diffusion MRI data with the application in lifespan neurodevelopment. I am also collaborating with <a href="https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=soMcNA8AAAAJ&hl=en" target="_blank">Dr. Ye Wu</a> developing the <a href="https://www.comedi.io/home" target="_blank">Computational Medical Imaging Toolkit</a> and <a href="https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=fE26FkMAAAAJ&hl=en&oi=ao" target="_blank">Dr. Yueh Lee</a> on accelerated F19 lung MRI.

I received my PhD degree in Biomedical Engineering from UNC in 2022 and bachelor degree in Biomedical Engineering from International University, Ho Chi Minh city, Vietnam. During my undergrad time, I worked with Dr. Loan Vo in designing MR compatible hardwares, designing experiments and acquiring data from studies' participants, and analyzing resting-state and task-based functional MRI.

**Fields of interest:**
- MR Physics and MR signal reconstruction/representation
- Diffusion MRI microstructure model
- Noise mapping and removal
- Neurodevelopment
- Functional and structural connectivity

**Academic services:**
- Journal reviewers: NeuroImage, PLoS ONE, IEEE-TMI, IEEE-TCDS.
- Conference/workshop reviewers: MICCAI, ISMRM, OHBM, MLMI.
